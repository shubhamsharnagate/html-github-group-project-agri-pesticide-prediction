👨‍🌾Welcome Farmers 👩‍🌾

Agriculture plays a vital role in our country. Farmers often face problems in selecting the correct pesticide for their crops. This system helps farmers choose the right pesticide based on crop and disease conditions.

Using the correct pesticide improves crop quality, reduces crop damage, and increases overall productivity.

🌟 Key Features

  • Simple and easy-to-use interface
  • Quick pesticide recommendations
  • Based on crop and disease type
  • Includes safety instructions
  • Farmer-friendly design

🌱 Benefits for Farmers

  • Improves crop yield and quality
  • Reduces misuse of pesticides
  • Saves time and effort
  • Supports better farming decisions
  • Encourages safe pesticide usage

📖 What is a Pesticide?

Pesticide
A chemical or biological substance used to protect crops from pests, diseases, and weeds. Proper use helps maintain crop health.

📌 Types of Crops

  1. Food Crops (Rice, Wheat)
  2. Cash Crops (Cotton, Sugarcane)
  3. Horticulture Crops (Fruits, Vegetables)

👨‍🌾 Farmer Safety Tips

Always wear protective gear | Avoid spraying during strong wind | Do not overuse pesticides | Keep away from children
